Setup Instructions
  1. Pair your TSP100IIIBI via iPhone Settings → Bluetooth
  2. Open your POS in Safari on your iPhone
  3. When you tap Print, Safari’s print dialog opens
  4. Select TSP100 from the printer list
  5. Tap Print — it prints via AirPrint over Bluetooth

The Star webPRNT Browser app provides its own local server at localhost:8080. Your POS will use that automatically when this mode is selected.
